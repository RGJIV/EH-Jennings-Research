# Timeline of Edward Henry Jennings (All Events From Iterations and Repo)

Focus: Pre-1930 events only.

- 1819: Father Richard Morley Jennings Sr. birth (expanded tree).
- 1831: Mother Katharine Evans birth.
- 1852: Born in Bradys Bend, PA (Find a Grave).
- 1855: First spouse Mary Jane Colwell birth.
- 1857: Sister Mary Katherine birth.
- 1861: Brother Richard Morley Jr. birth.
- 1864: Brother John Gundry birth.
- 1867: Sibling Jenifer Gundry birth/death.
- 1872–1891: Petroleum with father (early oil career).
- 1879: Married Mary Jane Colwell.
- 1880: Son Richard Gundry birth.
- 1887: Daughter Mary Brooks birth.
- 1891: Father death; co-founded Royal Gas Company; McDonald oil field.
- 1892: McDonald oil field development (Pittsburg Dispatch Dec 31, 1892).
- 1896: First spouse death.
- 1897-1898: Early WV oil mentions (e.g., Wheeling Register).
- 1898 (Post-Oct 3): WV oil leases (Tyler Co.; full list above; Sistersville Oil Review Oct 19, 1898).
- 1900-1906: NC real estate mentions (e.g., Semi-Weekly Messenger).
- 1903: Son Richard Gundry Yale graduation.
- 1905: Remarried Ella Bailey; NC lumber entry (Buncombe County timber).
- 1905–1906: PA leases (Zelienople).
- 1907: Mother death; brother Richard Morley Jr. death.
- 1910: PA holdings (Venango); arrested in Pittsburg graft scandal (conspiracy/bribery for depositories; president Columbia Bank, Pure Oil, Colonial Trust, Jennings Bros.; bail $10,000–$15,000; Spokane Press Jan 22, 1910); sentencing postponed with A.C. Griffin due to family illness (Palestine Daily Herald May 14, 1910).
- 1911: Purchased Toxaway properties ($100,000, NC lumber/resorts; NRHP Nomination).
- 1912: Brother John Gundry death; WV border fields.
- 1913-1919: NC newspaper mentions (Jackson Co. Journal; near Toxaway).
- 1914–1915: PA oil mergers (Allegheny); NC lawsuits (Smathers vs. Jennings; Jackson County Journal Apr 17, 1914).
- 1916: Dam failure (Aug 13; 5.3B gallons; hurricane rain; no draw-down; floods valley, destroys SC bridge 20-25 miles downstream).
- 1920: PA gas pipelines.
- 1921: Commissioners v. Jennings (NC Supreme Court; appeal from likely Transylvania/SC court; mistrial/new trial).
- 1922: MS mills.
- 1923: Died of pneumonia; funeral Nov 19 (Pittsburgh Gazette Times Nov 18, 1923).
- 1924: Grandson Richard "Jr." birth.
- 1925: PA refining facilities (The Daily Notes Sep 18, 1925).
- 1926: Grandson Donald birth.
- 1928: Grandson Melvin birth; PA gas exploration partnerships (The Morning Herald Apr 3, 1928).
- 1931: Son Edward Jr. death (Sep 3; suicide; obit mentions family businesses; Evening Star Sep 4, 1931); daughter Mary Brooks death.
- 1941: Son Richard Gundry death.
- 1944: Grandson Evan birth.
- 1970: Inez death.
- 2001: Grandson Melvin death.
- 2007: Granddaughter Ruth death.
- 2010: Grandson Evan death.
- 2017: Grandson Richard "Jr." death.
