## Generational Notes

- **Richard M. Jennings** deceased between 1905 and 1907 (widow Ella appears 1907 onward).
- **John G. Jennings** deceased between 1910 and 1914 (widow Katherine/Cathn appears 1914 onward).
- By 1914–1915 the operating leadership had shifted to Edward H. + next generation (R.G. and Edward H. Jr.).
